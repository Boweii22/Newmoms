About us

About us

Company History

The New Moms program, founded by Jenny, is dedicated to supporting young mothers and their children. This initiative provides comprehensive services, including housing, job training, and family support, to empower young moms to achieve stability and independence. By addressing the unique challenges faced by young mothers, the New Moms program aims to create a brighter future for both mothers and their children, fostering self-sufficiency and long-term success.

Mission & Vision

Mission & Vision

To empower young mothers through comprehensive support services, fostering independence, stability, and a brighter future for themselves and their children

A community where every young mother has the resources and opportunities needed to thrive, breaking the cycle of poverty and creating lasting positive change.

Latest News

We are excited to announce that the New Moms Program has expanded its outreach efforts to reach more families in need across our community. With the opening of two new support centers in underserved areas, we aim to provide vital resources, including housing assistance, job training, and childcare support, to an additional 200 young mothers and their children annually. This expansion is a significant step forward in our mission to empower and support young mothers, helping them achieve stability and independence for a brighter future. We look forward to continuing our impact and making a difference in the lives of more families in our community.

Our Team

Nikole Isaac

Founder & CEO

Jane founded the New Moms program with a vision to support young mothers in need. With over 5 years of experience in social work and non-profit management, she leads the organization with passion and dedication.

Wade Warren

Director of Programs

Cameron Williamson
