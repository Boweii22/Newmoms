About Newmoms

The New Moms program, founded by Jenny, is dedicated to supporting young mothers and their children. This initiative provides comprehensive services, including housing, job training, and family support, to empower young moms to achieve stability and independence. By addressing the unique challenges faced by young mothers, the New Moms program aims to create a brighter future for both mothers and their children, fostering self-sufficiency and long-term success.
